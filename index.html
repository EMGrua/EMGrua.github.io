Biography

Eoin Martino Grua is a Research Assisant at the University of Limerick (UL) at the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering. His current research focuses on the use of Machine Learning for the e-Health domain. He completed his Ph.D. research and will soon defend his thesis titled:‘The Future of E-Health is Mobile: Combining AI and Self-adapation to create adaptive e-Health mobile applications’. The Ph.D. research was conducted under the primary supervision of Prof. Dr. Patricia Lago and Prof. Dr. A.E. Eiben.
